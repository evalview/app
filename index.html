Features


At Eval-View we believe every candidate - and recruiter - deserves to present themselves at their absolute best. Our community of job-seekers and recruiters are here to help you rehearsing and prepare for your next job interview. You can practice interview with today's most popular and challenging interview questions in a safe, environment. Get feedback, reviews and advice from peers and professionals. Eval-View's community lets you practice and boost your confidence to help you ace your job interviews.

Interview Rehearsals

Choose from our expanding library of today's top interview questions. Practice, Record, & Post for Review.

Promote Skills

Show your skills by answering relevant questions designed to show your knowledge, experience and skills to peers and potential employers.

Community

Join and meet a community of other jobseekers, interviewees and the occasional HR professional or future employer where the objective is to get comfortable and get better at presenting the real you.

Your Portfolio

Present your highest ranking and most presentable self. After sharing, reviewing and practicing, you can build a portfolio of empowerment to show your best YOU!


About Us


At Eval-View, we are a group of continuous learners. We all have a wide range of backgrounds and skills. We have learned that no one person can be a master of all and that a community can share, teach and learn from each other. Out goal is to create a community of vast experience to share their learnings to help everyone present their best in the interview process.

Our Services


We are a community of continuous learners. Our services are about sharing.


Watch and Learn

Join and create a profile. Search skills and questions that you need to present. Watch and learn in a safe environment from others who are sharing their challenges and others that have mastered their presentations.


Rate and Review

You can't manage what you measure and our peer review environment provides a trusted measurement of your interview performance. Share and be reviewed by peers, seasoned professional, and HR recruiters. Get the feedback needed to present the best you.


Create and Present

Create your own profile, interviews and portfolio of skills. Refine and present your best interviews. Network with your for peer reviews and submit to our database of recruiters. We want to help you present the best you so you can download the job of your future.
